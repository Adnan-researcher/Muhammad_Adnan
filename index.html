About Me

I am an Artificial Intelligence researcher specializing in machine learning, computer vision, and autonomous systems. I hold a PhD in Interactive and Cognitive Environments from the University of Genoa (Italy) and Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (Spain).

My research focuses on LiDAR-based perception, object tracking, and ego-vehicle localization using probabilistic models and Dynamic Bayesian Networks. I am particularly interested in explainable AI, intelligent autonomous systems, and machine learning for real-world robotics applications.
